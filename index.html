RoboFeeder

A fully autonomous robotic system to perform in-mouth feeding for people with disabilities through object detection, facial keypoint recognition, and motion planning using the Kinova Gen 3 robot arm with RGB-D camera and ROS. Behavioral cloning with stochastic permutation was used to optimize the feeding trajectory.

A3C Vs. D4PG

This project evaluates and compares two Reinforment Learning models, A3C and D4PG, on the traditional continuous control problem in robotics. The training was performed on the Unity ML-Agent "Reacher" environment.
